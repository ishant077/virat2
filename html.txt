<font color="skyblue" size="8" face="cooper blank">Twinkle,Twinkle,Little star
</font><br/><br/><br/>
<font color="lightgreen" size="8" face="freestyle script"><p>Twinkle,twinkle,little star<br>
How i wonder what you are!<br>Up above the world so high,<br>
Like a daimond in the sky.</p><br>
<p>When the blazing sun is gone,<br>
when the nothig shines upon,<br>
Then you show your little light,<br>
Twinkle,twinkle,all the night.</p><br>
<p>then the traveller in the dark,<br>
thanks you for your tiny spark,<br>
he could not see which way to go,<br>
if you did not twinkle so.</p><br>
<p>in the dark blue sky you keep,<br>
And often thro, my curtains peep,<br>
for you never shut your eye,<br>
Till the sun is in the sky.</p><br>
<p>'Tis your bright and tiny spark,<br>
Lights the trav'ller in the dark,<br>
Tho' i know not what you are,<br>
Twinkle,Twinkle,little star.</p><br></font>
